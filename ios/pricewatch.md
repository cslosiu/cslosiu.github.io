### Description
```
Price Watch helps you compare everyday product prices across major Hong Kong supermarket and personal-care chains — so you can spot a better deal before you shop.

FEATURES
• Browse thousands of daily goods in clear categories
• Filter by category and subcategory
• Search by brand or product name
• Compare prices from different shops, listed from low to high
• See discount offers for each store when available
• Favourite products for quick checking next time
• Use downloaded data offline; refresh anytime to update
• See when prices were last updated
• Light and Dark appearance, and English / Traditional Chinese UI based on your device language

DATA SOURCE
Price information comes from the Consumer Council’s Online Price Watch open data (網上價格一覽通), published at:
https://online-price-watch.consumer.org.hk/opw/opendata/pricewatch.json

The dataset is typically updated daily and includes prices and offers from chains such as Wellcome, PARKnSHOP, Market Place by Jasons, Watsons, AEON, DCH Food Mart, and other retailers in the published data.

DISCLAIMER
This app is not an official Consumer Council product. Prices and offers are shown as provided by the open data source and may differ from in-store or online prices. Always confirm the latest price and offer terms at the retailer.
```
